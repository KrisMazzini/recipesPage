Cheesy Recipes for Students, by Kristopher Mazzini

The recipes below can help you don't die from hunger during a whole year living abroad in an exchange program.

Fake Carbonara

It was supposed to be a Spaghetti Carbonara, however I'm nowhere close to have the necessary skills for that, so I used my creativity. It's good enough to impress your friends though.

Ingredients

  • 350g of spaghetti
  • 150g of pancetta (or bacon)
  • 100g of blue cheese
  • 100g of any other cheese you like (i use Gouda or Mozzarela)
  • 4 eggs, or not
  • 150ml of heavy cream
  • salt and water

Steps

  1. Put a large saucepan of water on to boil. If the whole spaghetti does not fit in, there's something wrong.
  2. When the water starts boiling, put the spaghetti to cook and leave it until it looks eatable.
  3. Put salt in the boiling water accordingly to your taste. BE CAREFUL.
  4. Chop the 150g of pancetta as finely as you can, or simply buy one that's already chopped.
  5. Fry the pancetta, it does not taste good raw.
  6. Chop the blue cheese and the other cheese you've chosen, then mix them.
  7. In another pan, put the heavy cream to boil with the cheese mix and more salt. Do not put it in the same pan as the boiling water.
  8. Stir the heavy cream until the cheese is fully melted, then you have your sauce.
  9. Lift the spaghetti from the water and put it in the sauce, along with the pancetta.
  10. If you want eggs, scramble them in a frying pan and add it to the mix.
  11. Use a plate and a fork to eat.

Additional Info

  • You can use this recipe in larger scales, depending on how much leftovers you want to keep in the fridge.
  • For vegetarians, the pancetta can be removed from the recipe and it will still be great.

Scrambled Cheese With Eggs

It's just scrambled eggs, but if you put more and more cheese, it tastes better.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 300g of cheese
  • 200g of beef, chicken or sausage
  • salt and bread

Steps

  1. Just scramble the eggs with all the cheese you got.
  2. Add salt accordingly to your taste.
  3. If you think that you'll still be hungry, prepare some meat as well: just cook it with salt, it doesn't have to taste good, you'll just mix it with the eggs.
  4. In case you don't have or don't eat meat, use bread instead.
  5. If you also don't have bread, just make more eggs and use more cheese.
  6. Don't have it? I can only be sorry then.

Additional Info

  • It's a good meal for breakfast, lunch and dinner.
  • It's the better choice if you're too lazy to prepare the Fake Carbonara.
